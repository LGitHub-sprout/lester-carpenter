Trail Reviews

Northridge Loop

posted on January 17, 2014 by Daniel Weston

Trail Info

Trail Type
Single track
Elevation
2,000 ft.
Length
14.5 miles
Skill level
Beginner to intermediate

If you're looking for great scenery and a great workout without all the technical difficulty of other trails, Northridge Loop is for you. The ridge runs eat to west, with Ojai Valley on one side and Rose Valley on the other. The trail features amazing views of both valleys plus the Los Padres National Forest.
